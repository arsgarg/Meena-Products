Meena Products is a renowned manufacturer and supplier of a wide range of Gota Material. These products are widely appreciated for their fine finish, intricate design, colour-fastness and skin-friendliness.

About Us

Meena Products is one of the most trusted pioneer enterprises manufacturing Gota laces and allied goods over the years. We have gained immense trust among its customers. It was formed in 1964 under the dynamic leadership of late Mr. Suraj Narain Khatri Founder of the firm & continues to grow embracing new challenges, expanding boundaries and creating new opportunities. We are the leading manufacturers and dealers of all Gota Patti, Champa, Gota Phool, Lampi and all type of Gota Product.We are ultimate provider of every kind of material in gota under one roof wherein exporters and buyers do not have to go hunting elsewhere. We have won the hearts of numerous buyers throughout the India by providing high quality products, competitive price and timely delivery. . We are constantly working on developing new lines that the matches the design specifications and requirements of our new clients. We welcome you to send us your specification and requirements. Looking forward to serve you.

- Mrs. Meena Khatri
